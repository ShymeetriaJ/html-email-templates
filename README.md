# HTML Email Templates

A collection of three professional HTML email templates built with 
table-based layouts and inline CSS for maximum compatibility across 
all major email clients including Gmail, Outlook, and Apple Mail.

---

## Templates Included

### 1. Law Firm — Godfrey & Associates
A formal, professional template featuring navy blue and gold branding 
designed for legal correspondence.

### 2. Real Estate — Prestige Realty Group
A luxury template featuring dark charcoal and gold branding with an 
accent banner designed for property communications.

### 3. Medical Office — Riverside Medical Center
A clean, trustworthy template featuring blue and white branding with 
an appointment information box designed for patient communications.

---

## Why Table-Based Layouts?

These templates use HTML tables instead of modern CSS techniques like 
flexbox or grid. Email clients like Outlook use Microsoft Word's rendering 
engine which does not support modern CSS. Table-based layouts ensure 
consistent rendering across every email client.
